Netflix Dashboard

A Tableau Dashboard that analyzes Netflix's content since between the years 2008 and 2019.

The Dashboard


This dashboard was developed from this Kaggle Dataset, which explores the content Netflix has been producing between the years 2008 and 2020. A good follow up project would be developing a dashboard for the years after 2020, to see whether or not Covid had an impact on Netflix's business model.





Key takeaways:

• Movies have always been the main source of entertainment Netflix releases every year.

• Documentaries and stand up comedies are produced by Netflix much more than regular TV shows, which might be surprising but does make sense considering the lower budget and production time required when compared to traditional movies/TV shows.

• TV-MA and TV-14 shows constitute more than 5 times the number of TV-PG rated shows Netflix produces. The gap between R rated, PG-13, and PG rated movies os much less, indicating that rating plays a heavier role in a show's success compared to movies.

• Similar to the cinema industry, the US and India have been the biggest contributors to Netflix in terms of shows created.
